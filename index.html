Our Story

Born from passion.
Perfected by fire.

Nestled in the village of Coolaney, just 20 minutes from Sligo town, Bella & Brava is a family-run restaurant opened by Danny and Laura during the most unlikely of times — the 2020 pandemic. Against all odds, they built something extraordinary.

Every pizza is crafted to the exacting standards of the Associazione Verace Pizza Napoletana — using slow-proven dough made with 5 Stagioni 00 flour, San Marzano tomatoes, and buffalo mozzarella, then finished in a hand-built wood-fired oven at 450°C.

From crispy arancini to tagliatelle frutti di mare, from the panna cotta to the tiramisu — every dish is cooked to order from the freshest ingredients. This is not just a meal. It's an experience people drive from across Connacht for.

The Experience

More than a meal.
A destination.

  • 🔥

    Wood-Fired Oven at 450°C

    Hand-built on-site, reaching temperatures that produce the authentic char and leopard-spotted crust of true Neapolitan pizza.

  • 🌿

    Everything Made Fresh Daily

    All dough slow-proven on the premises. Every dish cooked to order. No shortcuts, no compromises.

  • 🍷

    Curated Wine Selection

    Portuguese Douro whites, Italian reds, Peroni on draft — a thoughtful list to complement every dish.

  • 🌡️

    Indoor & Heated Outdoor Dining

    Beautifully decorated interior plus a heated outdoor terrace — perfect for Sligo's unpredictable evenings.

  • 🤝

    Warmly Family Run

    Laura front of house, Danny in the kitchen. Guests from around the world return for the food — and stay for the welcome.
